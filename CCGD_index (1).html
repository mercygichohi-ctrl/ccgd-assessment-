Understanding Your Interests

💡 Why does this matter for you?
In Grade 9 and 10 you have a chance to choose a CBE pathway — STEM, Social Sciences, or Arts and Sports Science. That choice shapes your Senior School subjects and opens the door to your future career. This inventory is one step to help you choose wisely. Use it together with guidance from your teacher or CCGD counsellor.
Before You Begin

Informed Consent

Please read the information below carefully before agreeing to take part. This inventory is voluntary and your information will be kept private.

1
What is this assessment?

This is the CCGD Career Guidance Assessment Tool — a 42-item interest inventory based on John Holland's RIASEC model, adapted for Kenya's CBE Senior School framework. It is designed to help Grade 9 and 10 learners understand their natural interests and make informed decisions about their Senior School pathway and subject selection.

This is not a test. There are no right or wrong answers. Your results reflect your interests at this point in time and are meant to guide a conversation — not to make a final decision for you.

2
What will happen to my information?
  • Your name, school, and responses will be used only to generate your personalised results report.
  • Your information will not be shared with other learners, posted publicly, or used for commercial purposes.
  • CCGD Kenya may use anonymised, aggregated data (with no names attached) for research and curriculum development purposes.
  • Your results may be shared with your teacher or school counsellor to support your pathway guidance conversation.
  • You may request to see or delete your information at any time by contacting CCGD.
3
Your rights
  • Participation is completely voluntary. You are free to stop at any time without any consequence.
  • You do not have to answer any question that makes you uncomfortable.
  • Your pathway recommendation is a guide, not a final placement. You retain the right to choose your own pathway in discussion with your parents, teachers, and counsellor.
  • If you have any questions about this assessment, contact CCGD at info@careerguidancecollege.com or call +254 743 822 925.
